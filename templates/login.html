About this web app:


This is a web application that allows a staff member to view the segmented histopathological images
of a patient from which cell nuclei size information have been extracted. From this distribution,
while looking at the current treatment plan of dose, we may decide to change the course of treatment
for variables such as fractionation, dose per fraction, treating without chemo, etc.
